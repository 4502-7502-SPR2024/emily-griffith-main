7 Best Local Shops in Downtown Columbia

Columbia has a bustling arts community, to include a number of small businesses that sell locally-made products. This is a list of the seven best local shops in downtown Columbia, in my humble opinion.

    1. Poppy

    Poppy offers hand-made goods, gifts and art that are unique and personal.
